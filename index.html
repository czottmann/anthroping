(Unofficial) Anthropic Status on Bluesky & Mastodon

Anthropic Status publishes the current health of their various systems.

Useful, and you can get these updates via mail, text message, Slack message, webhooks, and RSS/Atom feeds. Notably absent: Accounts on "my" social networks, i.e. Bluesky and Mastodon.

So I made those accounts below. They're fed by the official webhook, so new messages go out as soon as updates happen.

  • Mastodon: @AnthroPing@mastodon.social
  • Bluesky: @AnthroPing.zottmann.dev
